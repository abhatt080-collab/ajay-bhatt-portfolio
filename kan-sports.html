CONTENT DEVELOPER

As a Content Developer at Kan Sports World Entertainment Pvt. Ltd., I was responsible for managing all digital media and content operations across the company's platforms — YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and all other social media channels. I handled end-to-end content for their sports events, from pre-production planning to post-production and final upload across all platforms.

I led a 6-member content team and reported directly to the company's Director.
