ABOUT CAMERAWORX

Cameraworx is a professional sports photography agency founded and led by Pradeep Mandhani — one of India's leading sports photographers. The agency specializes in high-pressure live sports and broadcast photography, working closely with India's top sports broadcasters including Star Sports, Sony ESPN, Ten Sports, and DA Sports. Cameraworx has covered some of India's biggest sporting leagues and international tournaments, delivering world-class sports imagery across disciplines.
