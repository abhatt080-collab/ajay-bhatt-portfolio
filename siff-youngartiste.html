WHAT IS SIFF YOUNG ARTISTE?

SIFF Young Artiste is a free online learning platform that offers music, dance, and instrument tutorials for students of all ages. With expert instructors and weekly live classes, SIFF Young Artiste nurtures the artiste in every student — completely free of charge.

MY CONTRIBUTION

As a Senior Content Developer at SIFF Young Artiste, I played a key role in building and scaling multiple YouTube channels from the ground up. From conceptualizing shoots to directing on-ground production, I ensured every piece of content met the highest quality standards. My work spanned across video editing, shoot coordination, thumbnail strategy, and content planning — helping channels like SIFF Guitar and SIFF Keyboard grow to over 800K subscribers each.

Beyond YouTube, I produced several high-impact video reels for Instagram that went massively viral — generating millions of views and significantly boosting SIFF Young Artiste's social media presence across platforms.
