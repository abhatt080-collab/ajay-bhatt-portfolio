ABOUT THE CHANNEL

PUC Parikshe Commerce is your ultimate learning destination for Karnataka PUC Commerce students. After empowering thousands of SSLC students, PARIKSHE launched this dedicated online platform for 1st & 2nd PUC Commerce students (KSEAB syllabus) — helping them master Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Statistics with ease and confidence, entirely in Kannada through expert-crafted content.

MY CONTRIBUTION

CONTENT MANAGER (SINCE 2024)

As Content Manager for PUC Parikshe Commerce since 2024, I lead end-to-end content operations — growing the channel to 45.1K subscribers and 1,821,018 views with 674+ high-quality educational videos. My role spans YouTube strategy, live and recorded class workflows, faculty coordination, production planning, team management, QC, analytics tracking, SOP creation, and campaign support.

I planned and coordinated the App Live workflow for Parikshe — designing the end-to-end process for how live classes go from studio to students via the Parikshe app. This included planning the Zoom-integrated live session flow, coordinating between the tech team, OLFs, and studio, and creating SOPs for app-based live execution so sessions run smoothly without technical failures.
